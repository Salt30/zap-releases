How It Works
Three simple steps to use Zap
Step 1: Activate
Press Alt+1 (or any mode hotkey). Zap captures your screen and shows an invisible overlay.
Step 2: Select
Drag to select any area on screen. Zap reads the text using OCR and sends it to AI. Or press Tab to type a question manually.
Step 3: Get Results
The AI response appears instantly in a floating panel. Copy it, or press Esc to dismiss and keep working.
● Stealth Mode Built-In
Truly Undetectable
Zap runs silently in the background with no dock icon. When you dismiss the overlay, it disappears completely — but stays ready. Your hotkeys work globally, so you can summon Zap from any app instantly.
Phantom Mode
Enable Phantom Mode in Settings to prevent screen recording software from capturing the Zap overlay. Perfect for screen shares and presentations.
Always Running
Zap lives in your menu bar and runs in the background. Press your hotkey anytime to bring it back — it's always one keystroke away.
