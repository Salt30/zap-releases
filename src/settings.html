Lockdown Mode
Enable Lockdown Mode
Use Zap alongside lockdown browsers like DigiExam, Respondus, or ExamSoft
How to use Lockdown Mode:
1 Enable Lockdown Mode and press Save Settings
2 Open Zap and activate the overlay (Alt+1 or your hotkey)
3 Then open your lockdown browser / exam app
4 Use stealth hotkeys to control Zap during the exam (see below)
5 When done, press Escape to hide the overlay, then close the exam app
